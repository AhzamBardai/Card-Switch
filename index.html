How To Play

Welcome again to Card Switch!
In this game you have to match each card by flipping 2 of the same cards! If you match them, then you earn points. If you get them wrong, you lose points. Simple right?
⇛Easy gets 5 points for correct and -1 for incorrect
⇛Medium gets 10 points for correct and -2 for incorrect
⇛Hard gets 20 points for correct and -5 for incorrect
